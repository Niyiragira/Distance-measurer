Distance measurer

Welcome!

Measure distance with accuracy higher than 50 meters!
To measure the distance(displacement) from your origin location to your destination location, proceed below:
  1. Turn on location on your device.
  2. When asked to know your location, click "Allow".
  3. Tap "Start" when you are at your origin location.
  4. Tap "End" when you reach your destination location.
  5. The value indicated between "Start" and "End" is the distance from the two locations in meters(m).
  • You must be having an internet connection at the start.
  • You must be having an internet connection at end.
  • Your cordinates are indicated at the button you tap.
N.B: This app measures only the displacement from your start point to your end point, not the length of the path taken!
